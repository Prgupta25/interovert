MOTIVATION:
    Managing bookings and events can be a complex and time-consuming process.
    Interovert was built to simplify and streamline the booking experience by
    providing a modern, intuitive web platform. The goal is to offer users a
    seamless way to browse, book, and manage events or services, while giving
    administrators powerful tools to manage listings, track bookings, and
    communicate with users via automated email notifications.
